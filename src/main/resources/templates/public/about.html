What We Do

Furnace Construction & Installation

Complete assembly of glass melting furnaces, from foundation to commissioning. We work with high-temperature refractory materials and precision steel structures.

Mechanical & Assembly Work

Locksmith work, pipe fitting, structural steel assembly, and heavy equipment installation. Our crews handle projects that require both precision and physical capability.

Maintenance & Technical Support

Scheduled maintenance, emergency repairs, equipment upgrades. We keep furnaces operational with minimal downtime.

How We Work

Safety First

Working with high-temperature equipment and heavy machinery requires strict safety protocols. Every team member is trained, every procedure is followed.

Precision Matters

Furnace construction isn't forgiving. Measurements must be exact, materials properly installed, timing coordinated. We don't cut corners.

Team Coordination

Complex projects need experienced crews working together. Our teams communicate clearly, solve problems on-site, and deliver on schedule.

International Projects

We operate across borders. Teams move between Poland and Portugal, working with different facilities, languages, and technical requirements.
